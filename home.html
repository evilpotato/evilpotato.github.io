Welcome to the NTT CALNET Web Portal

NTT, a leading global technology services provider, is honored to be a supplier for the CALNET Data Networks and Communication Services (IFB-C4DNCS19) contract. NTT products and services in CALNET will help organizations accelerate teamwork, create meaningful employee experiences and provide seamless collaboration across distributed teams.

For more than 28 years, NTT has worked together to help organizations around the world, which includes 16 years serving the State of California and all the various contracts, harness the power of technology to innovate. We are experts in VoIP services having deployed over 20 million VoIP devices and currently manage and support over 600,000 VoIP devices across the globe. For Microsoft Teams/Skype for Business, we have deployed 2 million seats and currently manage more than 1 million seats globally.

This Web Portal is your resource for all information related to NTT products and services offered through CALNET. These services include: Standalone VoIP (Category 21) and Hosted VoIP (Category 22). Visit this web portal to open trouble tickets, view your invoices, access reports and much, much more.
